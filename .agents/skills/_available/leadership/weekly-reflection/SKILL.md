# weekly-reflection

Weekly synthesis of progress and priorities

## Purpose

Weekly leadership reflection to maintain strategic focus and learn from the week.

## Usage

- `/weekly-reflection` - Review current week (Friday/Sunday)

---

## Steps

1. **Review week's activities:**
   - Search 00-Inbox/Meetings/ for this week's meetings
   - Check 03-Tasks/Tasks.md for completed items
   - Read daily plans/reviews

2. **Assess progress:**
   - Which pillar got most attention?
   - Key decisions made
   - Important conversations
   - Progress toward goals

3. **Identify patterns:**
   - Wins this week
   - Challenges encountered
   - Energy/motivation levels
   - Time well spent vs. wasted

4. **Set next week focus:**
   - Top 3 priorities
   - Key meetings
   - Strategic focus area

---

## Output Format

```markdown
# Weekly Reflection: [Week of Date]

## This Week's Focus
**Primary pillar:** [Which pillar got attention]
**Key outcomes:** [Top 2-3 results]

## Wins
1. [Win 1]
2. [Win 2]

## Challenges
1. [Challenge 1]
2. [Challenge 2]

## Key Decisions
- [Decision 1]
- [Decision 2]

## Next Week Focus
1. [Priority 1]
2. [Priority 2]
3. [Priority 3]

## Pattern Notes
[Any patterns worth noting]
```
